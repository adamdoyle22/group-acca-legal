Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Group Acca ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

Group Acca is operated by a UK-based team. The App is designed for users based in the United Kingdom.

Contact email: hello@group-acca.com
ICO Registration: Registration in progress

We recommend registering with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) before your public App Store launch. Registration costs £40/year and can be completed at ico.org.uk.

2. What Group Acca Does

Group Acca is a social coordination and bet tracking application. It allows groups of friends to organise group betting activities, make team picks, and track the success of their picks and bets over time. The App does not facilitate, process, or intermediate any financial transactions. Any betting or payments associated with group activities take place entirely outside the App through third party platforms chosen by the user.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and use Group Acca:

  • Name — collected at registration and used to identify you within the App and to other members of your private groups
  • Email address — collected via Apple Sign In and used to create and manage your account
  • Apple ID information — when you sign in using Apple Sign In, Apple may share your name and email address with us in accordance with Apple's own Privacy Policy. You may choose to hide your email address using Apple's private relay service
  • Username — a display name you choose during onboarding, visible to other members of your private groups
  • Monzo username (optional) — if you voluntarily provide this, it is stored solely to enable a deep link to the Monzo app to facilitate any payments between users outside of Group Acca. We do not access, store, or process any Monzo account credentials, payment details, or financial data
  • Device token — collected when you grant permission for push notifications, used solely to send you in-app notifications such as group updates and results
  • Activity data — your team picks, bet selections, and historical results within your private groups are stored to provide the core tracking and gamification features of the App

4. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be transparent about what we do not collect:

  • We do not process, hold, or have any visibility of any money transferred between users
  • We do not collect your Monzo account credentials or any banking or payment information
  • We do not collect payment card details
  • We do not place bets on behalf of users — any bets are placed independently by a group admin through a third party betting platform entirely outside the App
  • We do not share any of your data with betting operators or gambling platforms

5. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we must have a lawful basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

  • Contract performance — processing your name, email, username, and activity data is necessary to provide you with the Group Acca service and manage your account
  • Legitimate interests — to maintain the security and integrity of the App, send relevant push notifications, and improve our service
  • Consent — for optional data such as your Monzo username and push notification permissions, which you provide voluntarily and can withdraw at any time

6. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Group Acca account
  • To display your name and username to members of your private groups
  • To track and display your team picks, results, and betting history within your private groups
  • To send you push notifications about group activity, picks, and results where you have granted permission
  • To enable optional Monzo deep link functionality where you have chosen to provide your Monzo username
  • To communicate with you about your account where necessary
  • To maintain the security and performance of the App

7. Data Shared Within Your Groups

Group Acca is a social app. The following information is visible to other members of private groups you belong to:

  • Your username and display name
  • Your team picks and selections within the group
  • Your historical pick and bet success record within the group
  • Your Monzo username, if you have chosen to provide it

This information is only visible within the private groups you are a member of and is not publicly accessible outside of those groups.

8. Push Notifications

With your permission, Group Acca may send you push notifications relating to group activity, picks deadlines, and results. We collect your device token solely for this purpose. You can withdraw your consent for push notifications at any time through your device settings (Settings → Notifications → Group Acca). Withdrawing notification permission will not affect your ability to use the App.

9. Third Party Data Processors

We use the following third party services to operate Group Acca. These companies act as data processors on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements:

  • Supabase — we use Supabase as our backend database and authentication provider. Your account and activity data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase is GDPR compliant and stores data on servers located within the EU. You can review Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy
  • Apple — we use Apple Sign In for authentication and Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) for push notifications. Apple processes this data in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any other third parties for commercial purposes. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify you before any such sharing takes place.

10. Monzo Integration

Group Acca includes an optional feature that allows you to provide your Monzo username. This is used solely to generate a deep link to the Monzo app to make it easier for group members to settle any payments between themselves outside of Group Acca.

We wish to make the following clear:

  • Group Acca does not process, facilitate, or intermediate any financial transactions
  • Any payments between users occur entirely outside the App, directly through the Monzo application, which is governed by Monzo's own terms and privacy policy
  • We do not receive, store, or have any visibility of any payment amounts or transaction details
  • Providing your Monzo username is entirely optional and can be removed at any time from your profile settings

11. Age Restriction

Group Acca is intended for users aged 18 and over. The App is associated with group betting activities involving real money. By registering, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and associated data promptly.

12. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes. Specific optional data points such as your Monzo username and notification preferences can be removed or changed by you at any time within the App settings.

13. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration. Your data is stored on Supabase's secure, encrypted infrastructure. However, no method of data transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

14. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can request that we correct any inaccurate data we hold
  • Right to erasure — you can request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to restriction — you can request that we restrict how we process your data
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to data portability — you can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@group-acca.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk.

15. International Data Transfers

Our team operates across both the United Kingdom and the United States. Where personal data is accessed by team members based in the US, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with UK GDPR requirements for international data transfers, including reliance on standard contractual clauses where applicable.
